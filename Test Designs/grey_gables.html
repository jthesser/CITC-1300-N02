Grey Gables Bed and Breakfast - Rugby Tennessee

LINK: Grey Gables Bed & Breakfast Inn

Do you love gourmet food (breakfast and dinner include), a peaceful environment, comfortable bed and the possibility of making new friends than this bed and breakfast is just for you. Owner, Linda loves to provide her guests a homemade, full course meal and a relaxing environment for weary travelers. The lodge is located within a short distance from the Historic town of Rugby Tennessee which is a great place to spend a day and admire the days of old Victorian style housing and events.
