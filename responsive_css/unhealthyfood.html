Processed Foods and Fast Food Restaurants

  • US News - Is Fast Food Cheaper Than Cooking At Home?
  • Diet Meal Planner - Total Carbs in a McDonalds BigMac.
  • CalorieKing Carb counter book

Processed foods such as Fast food and most boxed foods in stores are really not healthy! They are often full of sodium, high in fat, and low in the vitamins and nutrients needed for a healthy lifestyle. Low fat is usually traded for higher carb count. Sugar Free is really not sugar free. It can be very hard to eat healthy in our society. The cost to purchase processed foods are often much cheaper than getting healthier foods from a local farm or deli. When going grocery shopping, I was always told to stay around the outside edges of the store and avoid the center ailes.
