Mia

Meet our "Road Dog" Mia!

Mia found us about 7 years ago as an abandoned puppy approximately 10 months old. My wife and I were venturing in some back woods and she appeared out of nowhere. She was starving so bad you could see all of her ribs, she was full of ticks and scared. It took some coaxing, but we finally was able to get her close enough to give her food and water. We couldn't leave her, so we brought her home. Now she is a happy and healthy dog with a loving home. She enjoys going for rides and knows how to sit, shake, and high five. She shows her love to us daily and we would not be complete without her.

Meet Mia

Mia loves to go for car rides. She knows when it is "Saturday" and we are home together as a family. She loves our cat Kola and doesn't harm anything except flies.... she hates anything that flys!

One of Mia's favorite places to visit is Catoosa Wildlife Management area. She loves the creeks, and seeing animals as we drive around aimlessly in nature. She knows the words "deer", "turkey" and often detects the animals before we even see them.
