Mia

Meet our "Road Dog" Mia!

Mia found us about 7 years ago. My wife and I were venturing in some back woods and she walked upon us as a 10 month old puppy. Someone had dropped her off. She was starving so bad you could see all of her ribs, full of ticks and scared. It took some coaxing, but we got her close enough to give her food and water. We couldn't leave her, so we brought her home. Now she is a happy and healthy dog with a loving home. She enjoys going for rides and knows how to sit, shake, and high five. She shows her love to us daily and we would not be complete without her.
