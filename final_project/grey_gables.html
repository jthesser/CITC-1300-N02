Grey Gables Bed & Breakfast - Rugby TN


Do you love gourmet food (breakfast and dinner included), a peaceful environment, comfortable bed and the possibility of making new friends? Then this bed and breakfast is just for you! Owner, Linda loves to provide weary travelers a homemade, full course gourmet meal and a peaceful relaxing environment. The lodge is also located within a short distance from the Historic town of Rugby Tennessee which is a great place to spend a day and admire the days of old Victorian style housing and events.
