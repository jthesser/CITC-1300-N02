Catoosa Wildlife Management Area

From I-40, take exit 320, Genesis Road

Crossville, TN 38555

LINK: Catoosa WMA -

One of my all time favorite places to go is Catoosa Wildlife Management Area on Genesis Road, Crossville TN. This is a public area, free to roam during the day. The main roads are gravel and most are easy to travel in a 2WD vehicle. Back roads require an off terain vehicle. I have seen many wildlife varities of birds and other animals such as Deer, Coyotes, Rattlesnakes, turkeys, and even a bear once. What is known as the Devil's Breakfast Table area is a nice swimming area that is fairly secluded. The main way to access it is now through Wartburg entrance since the bridge is closed off Otter creek through Genesis Road.
