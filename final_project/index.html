Hidden Gems in Tennessee

We live in such a diverse world that no matter who you meet, or where you're at, you will never have the exact same experience more than once. Enjoy the moments while you have them and take photos to help remember them for years to come! Some people may enjoy video games, watching Netflix, or even making websites. ... I truly enjoy creating adventures while travelling with my amazing wife and "road dog", Mia. This website is about three hidden gems that have stolen our hearts.

Catoosa Wildlife

More than 800 acres of wildlife located about 8 miles off I40 in Crossville TN.

Grey Gables B&B

A fabulous Bed and Breakfast located within walking distance to Historic Rugby TN.

Loco Burro Mex

A necessary stop anytime we go to Gatlinburg, TN. Enjoy the rooftop patio!
