A few Hidden Gems in Tennessee

From The life and Travels of Mia and Family

We live in such a diverse world that no matter who you meet, or where you're at, you will never have the exact same experience more than once. I always try to enjoy the little moments while I have them and take photos to help remember them for years to come! Some people may enjoy video games, watching Netflix, or even making websites. ... I most enjoy creating adventures and experiences, travelling with my amazing wife and "road dog", Mia. This website is about three hidden gems that we visit often and have stolen our hearts. Hopefully you will get a chance to visit them yourself!

NOTICE: All photos are original property of the website owner.

1-Catoosa Wildlife

More than 800 acres of wildlife located about 8 miles off I40 in Crossville TN.

2-Grey Gables B&B

A fabulous Bed and Breakfast located within walking distance to Historic Rugby TN.

3-Loco Burro

A necessary stop anytime we go to Gatlinburg, TN. Enjoy the rooftop patio!
