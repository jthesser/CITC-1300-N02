A few Hidden Gems in Tennessee

Brought to you by Josh Hesser and Family

You will never have the exact same experience more than once. I always try to enjoy the little moments while I have them and take photos to help remember them for years to come! Some people may enjoy video games, watching Netflix, or maybe even making websites. ... I enjoy having adventures and experiences while travelling with my amazing wife and our "road dog", Mia. This website is about three hidden gems that we visit often and have stolen our hearts over the years. Hopefully you will get a chance to visit them yourself one day and share your experience with others!

1-Catoosa Wildlife

More than 800 acres of wildlife located about 8 miles off I40 in Crossville TN.

2-Grey Gables B&B

A fabulous Bed and Breakfast located on the outskirts of Historic Rugby TN. Be sure to ask about her cookbooks too!

3-Loco Burro

A necessary stop anytime we go to Gatlinburg, TN. Enjoy the rooftop patio!
