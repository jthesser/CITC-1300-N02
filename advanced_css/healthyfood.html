Healthy Foods

What is considered a "healthy food"?

A Healthy Diet is different for each person due to different body types, and depends on many other health variables such as food allergies, dietary restrictions or intolerances.

Click the plate below for more information on how to eat healthy.
The majority of your plate should consist of vegetables, meat, fruits and grains.
Sugars and Sweets should be very limited.

IT'S ALL ABOUT MAKING GOOD CHOICES!

  • CDC- Eating Healthy
  • Harvard School of Public Health - What Should I Eat?

As a healthy human we should all try to choose healthy foods for the majority of our diets. A balanced meal should contain approx 45 grams of carbohydrates per meal. A Twix candy bar, for example, has 34 grams of carbohydrates... that is almost a whole meal! A full dinner plate, with salad, steak, baked potato and condiments would be about the same amount of carbohydrates. It all depends on healthy food choices!

SALADS and NATURAL FRUITS

  • Taste of Home - Healthy Salad Recipes
  • Eating Well - Why you should eat fruit.

Salads with lots of color and vegetables are a healthy choice with many vitamins and nutrients. Add proteins such as chicken or ham as well as a delicious dressing and maybe even some fruit, for a delicious and healthy full meal! Fresh fruits and vegetables are highly recommended. The majority of your plate should consist of fresh fruit, colorfull vegetables and mixed protein
